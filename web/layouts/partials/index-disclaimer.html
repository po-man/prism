Welcome to PRISM. Our goal is to help donors and animal advocates/researchers make more informed decisions by providing objective, data-driven analytics for animal charities.

Please note this platform is in a public preview. All content, including financial data and impact metrics, is extracted by Artificial Intelligence (AI) from public documents. While we aim for high quality, the automated process means the data is not guaranteed to be 100% accurate or complete.

Think of this as a starting point for your own research. Always verify information with the original source documents before making any decisions.
